Low rainfall can severely impact crop yield by causing water stress. 
Farmers should use irrigation methods such as drip or sprinkler systems.

Low phosphorus levels reduce root development and crop productivity. 
Applying DAP fertilizer improves phosphorus content.

Low nitrogen reduces vegetative growth and affects yield. 
Use nitrogen-rich fertilizers like urea.

High temperatures can damage crops and reduce grain formation. 
Use heat-resistant crop varieties and proper irrigation.
