Crop: Wheat
Condition: Low Nitrogen
Impact: Reduced vegetative growth and yield
Solution: Apply urea or nitrogen-rich fertilizer

Crop: Rice
Condition: Low Rainfall
Impact: Water stress reduces yield
Solution: Use irrigation or water retention methods

Crop: Maize
Condition: High Temperature
Impact: Heat stress reduces grain formation
Solution: Use heat-resistant varieties or shading

Crop: Cotton
Condition: Low Organic Carbon
Impact: Poor soil fertility
Solution: Add compost or organic manure
